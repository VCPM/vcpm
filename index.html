Product Hour

What?

Product Hour is a free one-hour consultation with your product team and 1-2 of our experienced product managers (PMs). Our PMs are presently working or used to work in Product Management Roles at various successful startups and top tech companies (Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Amazon). We will help you tackle whatever is keeping you up at night - from a tricky user onboarding challenge to scaling your product. The minimal outcome of Product Hour is a Value Decomposition TreeTM (VDT) for your product. VDT is a measurement framework which describes your product's value dimensions and corresponding success metrics. It represents a basis for systematic validated learning about how your projects deliver on each value dimension and how important each value dimension is for maximizing user value at any given point in time.

Why?

Our vision is to enable data-based, value-centric product management to drive key business metrics sustainably.

We are developing a new framework for Value Centric Product ManagementTM (VCPM) that helps you make product decisions based on data about which features are most valuable to your users. Our gain from tackling real product challenges with VCPM in product hours is the continuous calibration of the VCPM framework. VCPM helps you systematically apply methods from Lean Startup, Design Thinking and Data Science at the right time to maximize user value.
